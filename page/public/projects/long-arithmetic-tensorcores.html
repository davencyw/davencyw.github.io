Accelerating Long Arithmetic with TensorCores

David Blum
April, 2026

Abstract

Modern GPU TensorCores are designed to accelerate dense matrix multiply-accumulate operations, yet their potential for accelerating long integer arithmetic, as used in cryptography, arbitrary-precision computation, and zero-knowledge proof systems, remains largely unexplored. This project investigates how TensorCore hardware can be repurposed to perform multi-limb integer arithmetic at high throughput, mapping the carry-propagation structure of long addition and multiplication onto the low-precision matrix engines available on NVIDIA GPUs.
